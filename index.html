about

Fiduciary Realty is a small Bermudian real estate agency operated by Heather Bottelli and Lea Kessaram. We strive to bring a highly personalized service to our clients. Having access to a wealth of real estate experience enables us to assist in devising innovative methods for achieving your real estate goals. We believe that each transaction has a life and soul of its own and our aim is to make each of our business dealings have a good outcome for everyone involved.

services

At Fiduciary Realty, we can help you with buying, selling or renting real estate in Bermuda. If you are trying to sell or rent your property, we can advise on market conditions and what constitutes a realistic expectation for your particular property. By having our hand on the pulse of island dynamics, we are able to assist in setting a realistic price to ensure your property moves quickly.

Successfully selling property involves ensuring that the legal title and associated property matters are in good order. Our experienced staff can refer you to the appropriate professionals to help gauge the status of your property and ensure there are no barriers when it comes time to sell.

As needs change throughout one's life, people often look to acquire a larger property while others are simultaneously looking to downsize. Our vast network allows us to match such individuals with a view to organizing suitable exchanges, achieving savings in time and money for our clients.
